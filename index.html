Housing: Supply & Demand

Darwin’s Tight Rental Market

Greater Darwin has faced a persistently tight market since 2020. Vacancy rates worsened toward the end of 2025 to levels well below 1%. This is a stark contrast to 2019 when vacancy rates for units were nearly 9%.
