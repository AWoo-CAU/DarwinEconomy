Northern Territory Outlook

The Decade of Gas Delivery.

The Northern Territory is shifting from a construction-led "Investment phase" to a high-margin "Export phase." Over the next five years, the transition from offshore builds to onshore commercialization will drive a historic 7.1% GSP growth spike.
